ABOUT ME

Originally from Bangalore - a silicon valley from Southern India, I am pursuing my Masters in Information Management and Systems at the UC Berkeley School of Information. An avid reader, foodie, photgrapher and engineer; I am equaly passionate about my food and work. My focus at the ISchool is to delve deeper into the field of Data Science, by complimenting my technical skills with diversity of the ISchool to gain an insight into how the vast pools of data around us can be used to develop solutions for problems that span domains and expertise.

I have an undergraduate degree in Computer Science from PES Institute of Technology, India and have worked with Philips Healthcare as a senior software engineer prior to joining the ISchool. Armed with considerable experience as a developer and working with different technologies, I am now looking to concentrate on gleaning information from data through different techniques.

My classes at the I School include: Natural Language Programming, Quantitative Research Methodology, Information Organization and Retrieval
